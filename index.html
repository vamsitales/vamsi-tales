Google Analytics Setup

Here's how to connect real Google Analytics to your website.

Step 1 — Create a Google Analytics account
Go to analytics.google.com → Sign in with your Google account → Click "Start measuring" → Enter your website name and URL
Step 2 — Get your Measurement ID
After setup, Google gives you a Measurement ID that looks like G-NT0CVTYKGH. Copy it.
Step 3 — Replace the placeholder in your website
Open your vamsi-tales.html file → Find G-NT0CVTYKGH (appears twice) → Replace both with your real ID → Save and re-upload to Vercel
Step 4 — What you can track
Once live you will see: how many people visit daily, which articles are most popular, which city/country readers are from, how long they stay on each page, and how they found your website.

About Us

The Story Behind
Vamsi Tales

Vamsi Tales was born from a simple belief: that India's culture, history, and traditions deserve to be told in full. Not just the famous stories everyone knows, but the hidden gems, the forgotten legends, and the everyday magic that makes India unlike anywhere else on Earth.

Every article follows a simple formula — a real story, an Indian connection, and a lesson that stays with you. Whether you are a student, a curious reader, a traveller, a foodie, or just someone who loves a good story — there is something here for you.

We are just getting started. New stories every week. From festivals to food, from ancient temples to tribal art — Vamsi Tales is your window into the soul of India. 🇮🇳
