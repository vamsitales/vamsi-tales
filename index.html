Google Analytics Setup

Here's how to connect real Google Analytics to your website.

Step 1 — Create a Google Analytics account
Go to analytics.google.com → Sign in with your Google account → Click "Start measuring" → Enter your website name and URL
Step 2 — Get your Measurement ID
After setup, Google gives you a Measurement ID that looks like G-NT0CVTYKGH. Copy it.
Step 3 — Replace the placeholder in your website
Open your vamsi-tales.html file → Find G-NT0CVTYKGH (appears twice) → Replace both with your real ID → Save and re-upload to Vercel

About Us

The Story Behind
Vamsi Tales

Vamsi Tales was born from a simple belief: that the world is full of stories worth telling. India's culture, history and traditions. Life lessons that stay with you. Travel destinations that take your breath away. Food that tells a story. Mysteries that keep you up at night. Technology changing the world around us. Motivation that pushes you to be better. The quiet wisdom of nature and animals. And the timeless truths found in spiritual traditions.

Every article follows a simple formula — a real story, a human connection, and a lesson that stays with you long after you finish reading. Whether you are a student, a curious reader, a traveller, a foodie, a nature lover, or just someone who loves a good story — there is something here for you.

We are just getting started. New stories every week. From ancient mysteries to modern technology, from Himalayan lakes to Andhra kitchens, from elephant herds to neem trees, from sacred texts to everyday life — Vamsi Tales is your window into stories worth telling. 🪷

Tell stories that inform, inspire and stay with you long after you finish reading.
